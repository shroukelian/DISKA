Frequently Asked Questions

How can I register as a merchant?
You can click on the "Merchant Panel" button on the homepage, upload the required documents (Commercial Register and Tax Card), and your account will be activated within 24 hours.
What is the minimum order value?
The minimum order varies by supplier, but generally, the minimum for wholesale orders is 1000 EGP.
Is Cash on Delivery available?
Yes, we offer Cash on Delivery (COD) service for all governorates, in addition to electronic payments and wallets.
